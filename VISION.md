# Strata Vision

## The Future of Work Is AI-Mediated

As AI tools become central to knowledge work, every professional will need software that understands:

- what they know
- how they work
- what they are improving at
- what tools they use best
- where they create value

Today, every AI session starts cold.

That should not happen.

---

## What Strata Believes

Users deserve:

- private ownership of their work intelligence
- AI tools that adapt to them
- continuous growth visibility
- portable capability identity
- control over what is shared

---

## Long-Term Goal

Strata becomes the intelligence layer between humans and AI tools.

A trusted system that helps software personalize around people while helping people understand and compound their strengths.
